Since November 2024, I am part of the consortium of the [DRAGON project](https://www.fondationbiodiversite.fr/en/the-frb-in-action/programs-and-projects/le-cesab/dragon/). The project aims at studying the response of European dragonflies to anthropogenic pressures to propose indicators for freshwater ecosystems.

A first step was to assemble a large database of opportunistic dragonflies occurrences across Europe, with the help of over 25 data providers (15+ million points). 

We are currently analyzing these data with occupancy models to map dragonflies occupancy trends over the last 25 years, investigate their drivers and use results to propose a biotic indicator for freshwater health.
